  • Sweet Potato Hash Browns

    Ingredients:
    • 1 Sweet Potato
    • 2 Eggs
    • Salt, Pepper and Oregano (for seasoning)
    Directions:
    1. Peel sweet potato and spiralize to prepare hash browns
    2. Coat the bottom of a frying pan and add the sweet potato hash to it
    3. Cook on medium to high heat, stirring to avoid sticking/burning, until the sweet potatoes start to brown.
    4. Keep sweet potatoes in the pan but take them off the heat.
    5. In a separate pan cook over-easy eggs: cook until the egg whites have been cooked and then carefully flip eggs to avoid breaking yolks.
    6. Transfer your sweet potatoes to a plate and place eggs on top
    7. Sprinkle with desired seasonings and enjoy!

  • Blueberry Banana Oatmeal

    Ingredients:
    • 1 Serving Quick 1 Minute Oats
    • 1 Handful of Blueberries
    • 1/2 Banana, sliced
    • 1/2 Handful of Almonds
    • Honey

    1. Follow the directions on the container of oats to cook them. I usually just heat up some water and then add it to the oats.

  • Frozen Fruit & Yogurt Muffins

    Ingredients:
    • 1 Container Silk Vanilla Yogurt (or yogurt of your choice)
    • A Handful of Blueberries
    • A Handful of Strawberries, sliced
    • Granola
    • Honey
    Directions:
    1. Mix Honey with Granola until it starts to stick together
    2. Fill each cup in the muffin tin about halfway full with granola/honey mixture
    3. Fill the rest of each cup with yogurt
    4. Top with blueberries and strawberries
    5. Place in freezer overnight. Take them out of the freezer for a breakfast or snack!

  • Turkey Burgers & Guac

    Ingredients:
    • 1 pkg Ground Turkey
    • Red Onion
    • Garlic Salt
    • 1 Avocado
    • Jalepeño
    • Lime Juice
    Directions:
    1. Form ground turkey into 4-5 burger patties.
    2. Dice red onion and add to burgers.
    3. Sprinkle garlic salt on top.
    4. Cook patties on stovetop or grill.
    5. Mash avocado to make guacamole.
    6. Add some diced red onion, a SMALL amount of diced jalepeño, lime juice, and a pinch of salt to the guacamole.
    7. Top a few burgers with the guac and freeze/save the other burgers for another meal.

  • Spinach, Mushroom & Goat Cheese Scramble

    Ingredients:
    • 1 Handful of Spinach
    • 2 Mushrooms, sliced
    • 2 Eggs
    • Goat Cheese Crumbles
    • Red Onion
    • Jalepeño
    Directions:
    1. Dice a small amount of red onion and jalepeño.
    2. Mix onion and jalepeño with eggs in a small bowl or cup and set aside.
    3. Add olive oil to a small frying pan and cook spinach and mushrooms until spinach has shrunk.
    4. Add egg mixture to the pan and scramble eggs until cooked through.
    5. Move to a plate and top with goat cheese crumbles. Enjoy!

  • Green Smoothie

    Ingredients:
    • 2 Handfuls Spinach, frozen
    • 1 Banana, frozen
    • 2 Handfuls Mango Cubes, frozen
    • 2 Handfuls Pineapple Cubes, frozen
    • Vanilla yogurt or chia seeds, if desired
    • 1/4 cup Almond Milk
    Directions:
    1. Add almond milk and spinach to blender. Blend on medium speed until smooth.
    2. Add banana and blend until smooth
    3. Add half of mango and pineapple cubes and blend until smooth. Then add and blend the second half.
    4. Add yogurt or additional almond milk if the smoothie is too thick or won't blend.
    5. Add chia seeds or yogurt if desired. Enjoy!
